About Food with Magic

How it started

We have been serving for four generations, originating in 1970. Its’ well-known meatballs are the favorite of Stockholm citizens and tourists for years, their modesty, tenacity, respect, and love add up to an amazing place to eat. With the tradition and culture, it holds in its remarkable history, this offers more than the classical meatball taste. It is in the heart of Stockholm, which makes it possible for you to discover other secrets of the city easily.


Circus opening

We wanted to bring back to life “Panem et Circenses (bread and circus)” which refers to the provision of free entertainment and free food provided by the senate of Imperial Rome to the masses in a good way. And, since 2015 we offering breath taking circus experience next to our delicious dishes.


Our Foods

Craving a cheeseburger, some pad thai, a bit of crispy chicken and a steak all at once? Everything is possible. Pick tapas-sized dishes from a large menu served on the historical signature plank – please go ahead and tell us if you can think of something better than that! Since our dishes are smaller in size you can easily fit in 5–6. Or 11.Who’s counting? We strive for the menu to have something for every craving and for every palate, which is why we always strive to be innovative and creative when shaking things up on the menu and introducing new dishes.


Our Drinks

Of course the drinks follow the great and fun Pincho Nation concept, which means that they are picturesque and tasty. We have it all – sweet, sour, bitter, cold, warm, fresh, fruity and with or without alcohol. We dare say that we have something for every taste and mood, and we often update the drink menu to fit the season. Count on our brilliant bartenders to always serve you something Pincholicious with a twist!
